Momentum is already building. One provision from the Senate companion bill (S. 2022) — the Adoption Tax Credit expansion recognizing tribal courts — was enacted into law on July 4, 2025 as part of the One Big Beautiful Bill Act. H.R. 7705 carries forward the remaining provisions that Indian Country needs. Bipartisan support exists. Now is the time to act.

What the Bill Would Do

Seven Provisions for Tribal Parity

A

Tax-Exempt Bond Financing

Repeals the Essential Governmental Function test — unlocking low-cost bond financing for economic development, energy, broadband, and more

B

New Markets Tax Credit

Creates a dedicated $175M annual credit allocation for tribal statistical areas to attract private investment

C

Low-Income Housing Tax Credit

Auto-qualifies tribal lands as Difficult Development Areas, unlocking the 30% basis boost for affordable housing

D

Pension Plan Parity

Allows tribes to maintain one retirement plan for all employees — ending the two-plan burden no other sovereign faces

E

Indian Employment Tax Credit

Makes permanent and strengthens the employer hiring credit — H.R. 7705 raises the wage cap to $30,000 per employee

F

Tribal Foundations & Charities

Ensures tribal-affiliated foundations receive the same treatment as state-government foundations for philanthropic giving

G

IHS Health Workforce

Gives Indian Health Service professionals the same loan repayment tax incentives as other public health workers
